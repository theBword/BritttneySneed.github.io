The Strategist

A content strategist who thinks in systems and writes with humanity.

I specialize in helping brands close the gap between what they offer and how their audience actually experiences that value. My work sits at the intersection of content strategy, audience psychology, and brand voice — translating complex ideas into communication that feels personal, trustworthy, and human.

I don't just write content. I design the systems that make content work. From 10 years building community strategy at AARP to lifecycle campaign leadership at PenFed Credit Union, I've delivered results across regulated environments, mission-driven organizations, and consumer-facing brands.

Why the Autism Care System Wasn't Built for Modern Families

How fragmented systems create invisible pressure on caregivers

The modern family is expected to navigate autism care like a full-time operations team. This piece argues that human-centered care isn't softer language or better branding — it's the recognition that emotional experience is part of caregiving. Clear communication and accessible education are now expectations, not extras.
